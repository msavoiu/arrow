# Arrow
Social media network created with the aromantic/asexual community in mind. Find friends, partners, discuss a-spec experiences, and find and organize events near you!

You can follow my development and progress on my [Notion page](https://kokeshidynamo.notion.site/Arrow-139be3c378ec8020b703f7e046a2f5f1)!

## About
As I've gotten older, I've noticed increasing emphasis being placed on romantic and sexual partnerships in a way that kind of relegates friendship to the sidelines.
The aromantic/asexual community can often feel the impacts of this mindset shift the hardest, as they often don't seek out and enter romantic and sexual relationships with others. This doesn't mean that they don't value friendship and connection with other people. They prefer to do it in a way that's a bit different from the norm—which is what Arrow was made for!
